Not at all!MindSpace is designed for anyone looking to check in with their emotions, reflect or de-stress - whether you're seeing a therapist or not. It can be a great standalone support tool.

Absolutely We do not collect or share your journal entries or mood data. Everything you write stays on your device unless you choose to back it up.
